# 🌍 Kenzai

**Simulate Your Impact. Shape Your Future.**

Kenzai is a personalized sustainability platform that helps users understand and reduce their carbon footprint by simulating the impact of their daily actions.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Kenzai transforms complex environmental data into an interactive and engaging experience.  
Instead of just showing statistics, it allows users to **experiment with their lifestyle choices** and instantly see how those changes affect their carbon footprint.

---

## 🎯 Problem

People are aware of climate change but lack clarity on how their daily actions contribute to it.  
Existing solutions are either too complex or not personalized, making it difficult to take meaningful action.

---

## 💡 Solution

Kenzai creates a **digital carbon profile (Carbon Twin)** of the user and provides:

- Real-time carbon footprint calculation  
- Interactive “What-if” simulation  
- Gamified dashboard with rewards  
- AI-powered sustainability suggestions  

---

## 🧩 Features

- 🔮 **Simulation Engine**  
  Instantly visualize how lifestyle changes impact carbon footprint  

- 📊 **Personal Dashboard**  
  View carbon score, breakdown, and rankings  

- 🎮 **Gamification**  
  Earn points, badges, and track progress  

- 🤖 **AI Assistant**  
  Get personalized suggestions for reducing emissions  

- 👤 **Carbon Twin**  
  A digital reflection of your environmental impact  

---

## 🏗️ Architecture

Kenzai follows a modular architecture:

- **Frontend:** React.js  
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express  
- **Simulation Engine:** Rule-based logic  
- **AI Layer:** Hardcoded JS dictionary 
- **Visualization:** MUI

---

## 🔄 How It Works

1. User enters lifestyle data (transport, diet, electricity, etc.)
2. System calculates carbon footprint
3. Dashboard displays score and breakdown
4. User simulates changes in habits
5. System shows real-time impact
6. AI provides personalized suggestions
